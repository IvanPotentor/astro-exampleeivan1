Our proven process

01

Kickoff Call

Your dedicated Marketing Strategist Manager will meet with you in person or virtual, to gain a full understanding of your business and overall goals to plan a successful marketing strategy.

02

Strategy Plan

Once we have completed studying your product or service we will align your goals to your budget. Then our team will investigate your marketplace, see what your competition is doing and find ways to help you do better. Once we review your marketing strategy plan, our team will begin the launch of your campaign based on your plan. You will be given the launch date and check-in calendar to follow along with the team. After approval for this step you became part of our Orange Dot MKT team.

Data and results

We will provide you monthly data, for us results are everything. Our purpose is to work together, meet your monthly goals and continue improving.
